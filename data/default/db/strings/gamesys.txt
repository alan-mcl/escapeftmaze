modifier_desc-cut: A sword stroke intended to wound with the edge of the blade.  Most swords have this attack mode.
modifier_desc-lunge: A sword stroke intended to wound with the point of the blade.  Most swords and daggers have this attack mode.

modifier_desc-punch: An unarmed attack carried out with the hand, upper arm or elbow.

modifier_desc-rhyme: Modifies spell effects that require a rhyme or verse component.  These are typically Witchcraft and Enchantment spells.
modifier_desc-gesture: Modifies spell effects that require a hand sign or gesture.  These are typically Black Magic and Sorcery spells.
modifier_desc-posture: Modifies spell effects that require a posture or dance component.  These are typically Enchantment and White Magic spells.
modifier_desc-thought: Modifies spell effects that require a purely mental component.  These are typically Sorcery and Elemental spells.
modifier_desc-herbal: Modifies spell effects that require herbal reagents.  These are typically Druidism and Witchcraft spells. Also governs attempts to create potions and powders from herbal components.
modifier_desc-alchemic: Modifies spell effects that require mineral, metal or similar reagents.  These are typically Elemental and Black Magic spells. Also governs attempts to create potions and powders from alchemic components.
